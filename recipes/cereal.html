Cereal

Description

Cereal is a breakfast staple around the globe. With only two ingredients you may be fooled into a false sense of security thinking you know the recipe by heart. Fear not! For this recipe seeks to preserve the sacred ritualistic preparation of the delicacy known the world over as none-other-than: cereal.

Ingredients

  • Cereal - 1.5 Cups
  • Whole Milk - .75 Cups

Steps

  1. Pour cereal into an appropriately-sized bowl.
  2. Add milk to bowl.
  3. Serve immediately. (Cereal will become soggy if not served in a timely manner!)
