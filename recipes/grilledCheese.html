Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled Cheese is a sandwich staple around the globe. With only three ingredients you may be fooled into a false sense of security thinking you know the recipe by heart. Fear not! For this recipe seeks to preserve the sacred ritualistic preparation of the delicacy known the world over as none-other-than: Grilled Cheese.

Ingredients

  • Bread - 2 Slices
  • American Cheese - 2 Slices
  • Butter - 2 Tbsp

Steps

  1. Place Cheese between bread.
  2. Melt butter in pan.
  3. Cook 2 minutes per side or until golden brown.
