Hotdog

Description

Hotdogs are a staple around the globe. With only two ingredients you may be fooled into a false sense of security thinking you know the recipe by heart. Fear not! For this recipe seeks to preserve the sacred ritualistic preparation of the delicacy known the world over as none-other-than: Hotdog.

Ingredients

  • Hotdog
  • Bun

Steps

  1. Heat Hotdog to desired temperature using your desired cooking method.
  2. Place Hotdog in bun.
  3. Serve with desired condiments.
